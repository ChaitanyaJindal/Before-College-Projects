About - TAS

The Adhyyan School, is a vision and mission of Shri Ram Niwas Ji who is the founder of R.S. Educational Society resolved to stipulate and specify quality education to children in a student friendly environment. We here endeavor and strive to fetch education by way of practice and authentic Adhyyan that is LEARNING OF THE MIND, BODY AND SOUL.

It has been imparting a holistic education to its lively students under the guidance of vigorous and dynamic Principal and Faculty Members.

The school has carved a niche for being progressive and distinctively matchless in terms of 360o development of children in its true sense. Our students have brought honor and praise in every field whether it is academics, sports, music-dance, drama or debate. The school has strengthened its skill base, has built upon its infrastructure and opened up a new panorama growth where children can nurture and revamp their creativity and exercise their imagination in myriad ways. It is vibrant institution blessed with a sprawling green campus, culture and values. All the teachers are highly qualified, young, enthusiastic and committed to the welfare of children. The Teachers do not merely teach, they touch the lives and teach how to live...

The progress of this institution will continue with the same intensity as has been dream by the hon'ble founder of THE ADHYYAN SCHOOL.

With the continuous efforts of the students, staff members, parents and support of management of the school, we are conclusive that we will steadily achieve its cherished goals. We, passionately, feel that the students under our care must attain all the opportunities to realize their skill and potentials in a secure and inspiring environment.
